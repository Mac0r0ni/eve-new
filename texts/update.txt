*** Automatic bot detection! (beta) ***
    Eve can now detect bots without captcha verification!
    Auto-bot detect system can detect almost all the bots.
    Captcha Verification can be performed for accounts less than 20 days by default and it can be customized by the owner.

*** Set custom verification days. (admin command)
	Say: Eve set verification days to <number>

	example: Eve set verification time to 5
	verification time will be set to 5 days. New person less than 5 days will have to verify.
